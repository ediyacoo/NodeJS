Gulf Electronic Scientific Research (GESR)

During the third meeting of the technical team responsible for establishing a database for the universities, higher-education institutions, and national research centers, held on 5-6 of July 2005 in Manama, Bahrain, Kuwait Institute for Scientific Research (KISR) was selected to be the hosting party for the GCC database project, which was later on officially named "Gulf Electronic Scientific Research (GESR)". An agreement was reached for GESR to include the following components: Organizations database: containing universities, higher-education institutions, and national research centers. Experts-manpower database: containing academics, researchers, and specialists in academic and research work. Research database: containing research, periodicals, articles, and conferences proceedings. Scientific-equipments database: containing lab equipments used for scientific research.
